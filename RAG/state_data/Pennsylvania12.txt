Topic: Interstate Agreements Streamlined Sales Tax Membership Status

Nonmember Pennsylvania is not a member of the Streamlined Sales and Use Tax Agreement. Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board, About Us; State Info. Topic: Interstate Agreements Streamlined Sales Tax Conformity to Agreement

Nonmember Pennsylvania is not a member of and does not conform to the Streamlined Sales and Use Tax Agreement. Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board, About Us; State Info. BNA-SUTN PA 2.1.2.

Topic: Interstate Agreements Multistate Tax Commission

Associate Member Pennsylvania is not a compact member of the Multistate Tax Commission (MTC). However, as an associate member, Pennsylvania may participate in Commission meetings and consult with the Commission and its other member states. Mulistate Tax Commission, Member States. In November 2014, Pennsylvania joined the MTC's joint audit program. Pennsylvania Tax Update No. 178 (Feb./March 2015) The purposes of the Multistate Tax Compact are to:• facilitate proper determination of state and local tax liability of multistate taxpayers, including equitable apportionment of tax bases and settlement of apportionment disputes; • promote uniformity or compatibility in significant components of tax systems; • facilitate taxpayer convenience and compliance in the filing of tax returns and in other phases of tax administration; and • avoid duplicative taxation. Multistate Tax Commission, Multistate Tax Compact, Article 1: Purpose. BNA-SUTN PA 2.2.
