Topic: Interstate Agreements Streamlined Sales Tax Conformity to Agreement

Nonmember Maine is not a full or associate member of the Streamlined Sales Tax and Use Tax Agreement and does not conform to the agreement. Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board, State Info. BNA-SUTN ME 2.1.2.

Topic: Interstate Agreements Multistate Tax Commission

Associate/Project Member Maine is an associate and project member of the Multistate Tax Commission according to information provided by the Commission. Multistate Tax Commission, Member States. Maine tax law relating to the Multistate Tax Compact has been repealed as of 2005; however, Maine continues to retain its status as an associate and project member. Me. Rev. Stat. Ann. tit. 36, ยง7105(repealed); Multistate Tax Commission, Member States. Associate member states may participate in Commission meetings, consult and cooperate with the Commission and the other member states, and participate in Commission programs or projects as a project member. Multistate Tax Commission, Definition of Member States; 2013 Me. H.P. 251, enacted June 5, 2013 (requiring the Office of Fiscal and Program Review to prepare a report concerning the SST). The report and any proposals for legislation to conform Maine's sales and use tax laws to the SST must be submitted to the Joint Standing Committee on Taxation no later than Jan. 15, 2014. BNA-SUTN ME 2.2.
