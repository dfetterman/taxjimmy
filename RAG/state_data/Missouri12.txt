State: Missouri



Topic: Interstate Agreements Streamlined Sales Tax Membership Status

Advisory Member Missouri is not a full member of the Streamlined Sales and Use Tax Agreement. Streamlined Sales Tax, State Info. However, it is an Advisory State (non-voting status) to the Governing Board and may give input on any matter presented to the Board. Mo. Rev. Stat. §144.1000 to Mo. Rev. Stat. §144.1015. BNA-SUTN MO 2.1.1.

==================================================

Topic: Interstate Agreements Streamlined Sales Tax Conformity to Agreement

Nonmember Missouri has not enacted legislation to bring Missouri into compliance with the Streamlined Sales and Use Tax Agreement at this time. Mo. Rev. Stat. §144.1000 to Mo. Rev. Stat. §144.1015. However, Missouri has enacted legislation allowing it to enter into the Streamlined Sales and Use Tax Agreement. Mo. Rev. Stat. §144.1000 to Mo. Rev. Stat. §144.1015. BNA-SUTN MO 2.1.2.

==================================================

Topic: Interstate Agreements Multistate Tax Commission

Compact Member Missouri is a Compact member state of the Multistate Tax Compact. Mo. Rev. Stat. §32.200. The Compact has been challenged;however, the U.S. Supreme Court upheld its validity under the Compact Clause of the U.S. Constitution. U.S. Steel Corp. v. Multistate Tax Comn., 434 U.S. 452 (1978). Missouri has signed the Uniform Exchange of Information Agreement. The purpose of the agreement is to enhance tax administration through the exchange of taxpayer information between state agencies who are parties to the agreement. Information subject to exchange between states includes, but is not limited to, lists of taxpayers, tax returns, nexus information and questionnaires, audit reports, collection activities, and appeals and criminal tax matters. Multistate Tax Commission, Uniform Exchange of Information Agreement, Article I. The Multistate Tax Commission has developed a Uniform Sales and Use Tax Certificate that 38 states have indicated is acceptable for use as a “blanket” resale certificate and for similar purposes. The Certificate itself contains instructions on its use, lists the states that have indicated to the Commission that a properly filled out form satisfies their requirements for a valid resale certificate, and sets forth specific limitations on its use. Missouri accepts this form. Multistate Tax Commission, Uniform Sales and Use Tax Certificate. BNA-SUTN MO 2.2.

Topic: Interstate Agreements Other Interstate Agreements

KS/MO Investment Compact Missouri has entered into the Kansas and Missouri Regional Investment District Compact. Mo. Rev. Stat. §70.515. The compact provides that, subject to voter approval, counties in the district may impose a sales tax in order to support regional programs such as a public transit system. Mo. Rev. Stat. §70.515. BNA-SUTN MO 2.3.
