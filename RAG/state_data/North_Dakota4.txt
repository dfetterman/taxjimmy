House trailers and mobile homes are excluded from the definition of “motor vehicle” and are subject to sales and use tax rather than motor vehicle excise tax. Travel trailers are included in the definition of ‘motor vehicle.' N.D. Admin. Code §81-05.1-01-03(11). Recreational travel trailers not exceeding eight feet in width or 32 feet in length designed to be principally used as temporary vacation dwellings and sold to residents of other states which impose excise taxes upon registration of such travel trailers are exempt from tax. Topic: Transportation Property, Fuel, Services Property and Passenger Transportation Property Transportation, Moving Services

Nontaxable North Dakota's statute and regulations governing sales and use tax do not specifically address moving services, but generally professional and personal services are not subject to tax in the state. N.D. Admin. Code §81-04.1-01-22. Nevertheless, materials of a permanent and reusable nature are subject to North Dakota sales tax when purchased by a moving or storage company. Materials intended for one-time usage are also subject to North Dakota sales tax when used to pack, preserve, load, or store a shipment from one point in the state to another. N.D. Admin. Code §81-04.1-01-22; N.D. Admin. Code §81-04.1-04-33. These materials may be exempt from sales tax if used to pack, preserve, load, or store a shipment from a point in North Dakota to a point outside the state. N.D. Admin. Code §81-04.1-04-33. BNA-SUTN ND 29.9.8.
