Topic: Interstate Agreements Streamlined Sales Tax Conformity to Agreement

In Compliance As a full member of the Streamlined Sales and Use Tax Agreement for which the Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board has not issued a notice of noncompliance, Rhode Island is considered to be in full conformity with the Agreement. Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board, Certificate of Compliance, Rhode Island (version 2018.0). On Oct. 25, 2017, the Compliance Review and Interpretations Committee issued a report finding that Rhode Island is not out-of-compliance with the Agreement. Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board, 2017 Compliance Review Report (Oct. 25, 2017); see also Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board, 2016 Compliance Review Report (Nov. 22, 2016). Topic: Interstate Agreements Multistate Tax Commission

Associate Member Rhode Island is an associate member of the Multistate Tax Commission. Multistate Tax Commission, Member States. As an associate and project member state, Rhode Island participates in Commission meetings and otherwise consults and cooperates with the Commission and other member states, and participates in Commission projects and programs. See Multistate Tax Commission, About the Multistate Tax Commission. BNA-SUTN RI 2.2.
