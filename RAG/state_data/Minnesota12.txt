State: Minnesota



Topic: Interstate Agreements Streamlined Sales Tax Membership Status

Full Member Minnesota became a full member of the Streamlined Sales and Use Tax Agreement on Oct. 1, 2005. See Minn. Stat. ยง297A.995;Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board, Certificate of Compliance, Minnesota(version 2018.0); Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board, Taxability Matrix, Minnesota (version 2018.0). Initially, Minnesota enacted provisions of the Streamlined Sales and Use Tax Agreement under previous versions of the agreement. 2001 H.F. 1, enacted June 30, 2001; 2001 H.F. 2498, enacted May 18, 2002, without the governor's signature. Under this legislation, Minnesota also created the 6.5 percent fur tax and scheduled the city sales tax authority to sunset December 31, 2005. On May 25, 2003, Minnesota enacted S.B. 1505 in order to bring the state into compliance with the full agreement. 2003 S.B. 1505, effective January 1, 2004; see also Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board, State Guide to the Streamlined Sales Tax Project (March 1, 2019). The Minnesota Department of Revenue provides information on its website regarding the state's membership in the Streamlined Sales and Use Tax Agreement (SSUTA) and streamlined registration instructions for sellers. Minnesota Dept. of Rev., Streamlined Sales and Use Tax. BNA-SUTN MN 2.1.1.

Topic: Interstate Agreements Streamlined Sales Tax Conformity to Agreement

In Compliance As a full member of the Streamlined Sales and Use Tax Agreement for which the Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board has not issued a notice of noncompliance, Minnesota is considered to be in full conformity with the Agreement. Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board, Certificate of Compliance, Minnesota (version 2018.0); see also Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board, User Guide: Online Taxability Matrix and Certificate of Compliance (Nov. 20, 2018). On Oct. 25, 2017, the Compliance Review and Interpretations Committee issued a report finding that Minnesota was not out-of-compliance with the Agreement. Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board, 2017 Compliance Review Report (Oct. 25, 2017); see also Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board, 2016 Compliance Review Report (Nov. 22, 2016). BNA-SUTN MN 2.1.2.
