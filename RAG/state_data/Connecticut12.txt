State: Connecticut



Topic: Interstate Agreements Streamlined Sales Tax Membership Status

Nonmember Although Connecticut is a voting participant of the Streamlined Sales Tax Project, it has not yet enacted legislation to comply with the Streamlined Sales and Use Tax Agreement. Connecticut Dept. of Rev. Svcs., Streamlined Sales Tax Commission Report. Therefore, Connecticut is not a member state. Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board, Streamlined State Status. BNA-SUTN CT 2.1.1.

==================================================

Topic: Interstate Agreements Streamlined Sales Tax Conformity to Agreement

Nonmember Connecticut is not a member state to the Streamlined Sales and Use Tax Agreement. Connecticut Dept. of Rev. Svcs., Streamlined Sales Tax Commission Report. For discussion of the streamlined sales tax system, see 1300.13 T.M., Sales and Use Taxes: Streamlined Sales Tax System. BNA-SUTN CT 2.1.2.

==================================================

Topic: Interstate Agreements Multistate Tax Commission

Associate/Project Member Connecticut is an Associate Member to the Multistate Tax Commission. See Multistate Tax Commission, Member States. BNA-SUTN CT 2.2.

Topic: Interstate Agreements Other Interstate Agreements

None Connecticut is not a member of other interstate agreements for sales and use tax purposes. Connecticut was once a member of the Cooperative Interstate Sales and Use Tax, an agreement between the states of New York and Connecticut that allowed participating businesses making sales across state lines to voluntarily collect the neighboring state's tax. The agreement also allowed the two states to actively exchange sales and use tax information. Connecticut Informational Publication IP 2006(11) (April 2007). As of August 2009, the simplified sales tax reporting program between New York and Connecticut was terminated. New York Notice N-09-16 (Aug. 2009). However, the provisions regarding the exchange of tax information were retained through an amendment to the Reciprocal Agreement of the Department of Revenue Services of the State of Connecticut and the Department of Taxation and Finance of the State of New York Providing for the Exchange of Sales and Tax Information, signed June 10, 1982. New York Notice N-09-16 (Aug. 2009). BNA-SUTN CT 2.3.
