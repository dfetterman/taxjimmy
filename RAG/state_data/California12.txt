Topic: Interstate Agreements Other Interstate Agreements

Border States Caucus/UEIA California participates in the Border States Caucus and the Uniform Exchange of Information Agreement. California Tax Publication 32. Border States Uniform Sales for Resale Certificate California, along with New Mexico, Arizona, Texas, and the United Mexican States have formed the Border State Caucus in order to promote trade in the Southwest Region of the United States. California will accept a Border State Sale for Resale Certificate from businesses operating within Arizona, California, New Mexico, Texas, as well as the United Mexican States. The Certificate is to be presented when the business purchases goods for resale across state or national borders. California Tax Publication 32.
