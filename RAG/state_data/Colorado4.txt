Topic: Transportation Property, Fuel, Services Motor Vehicles, Intrastate or Non-Commercial Use Short-term Motor Vehicle Rentals

Taxable Short-term motor vehicle rentals are subject to sales and use tax. Colo. Rev. Stat. §39-26-102(23); Colorado General Information Letter GIL-16-011 (July 6, 2016). Further, a forfeited deposit fee will be subject to sales and use tax if the deposit exceeds 50%of the daily rental charge. If the deposit is below 50% of the daily fee, it will be considered a nontaxable cancellation charge. Colo. Rev. Stat. §39-26-102(23); Colorado General Information Letter GIL-16-011 (July 6, 2016). The lease payments on a lease contract for a term of three years or less are exempt from sales tax if the lessor has already paid sales or use tax on the acquisition price of tangible personal property being leased. In lieu of paying tax on the acquisition price of property leased for terms of three years or less, a lessor may instead, upon permission of the Department of Revenue, collect sales tax on lease payments received for the rental of the property. Colo. Rev. Stat. §39-26-713(1)(a); 39 Colo. Code Regs. §26-713.1(a). To obtain permission to collect sales tax on the lease payments, the lessor must submit an application on DR 0440 to the Department of Revenue. Colorado FYI Sales 56. If permission is granted, the lessor's election to collect sales tax on the lease payments as they are made is binding upon the lessor with respect to all property that the lessor leases, regardless of the duration of the lease term. 39 Colo. Code Regs. §26-713.1(a); Colorado FYI Sales 56. If a lease or rental period exceeds three years, the lessor is required to collect and remit sales tax on the rental payments received from the lessee. Colo. Rev. Stat. §39-26-102(23); 39 Colo. Code Regs. §26-102.23. The lessor also is required to submit an application for a “Permit to Collect Sales Tax on the Rental or Lease Basis,” Form DR 0440. Colorado FYI Sales 56. BNA-SUTN CO 29.3.7.
