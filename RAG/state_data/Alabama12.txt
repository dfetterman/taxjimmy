State: Alabama



Topic: Interstate Agreements Streamlined Sales Tax Membership Status

Nonmember Alabama is an advisory member (non-voting member) of the Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board. Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board, Streamlined State Status. Alabama achieved advisory status through the passage of enabling legislation in 2002. Alabama is not a member state to the Streamlined Sales and Use Tax Agreement. 2002 Ala. S.B. 185, effective April 17, 2002; Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board, Streamlined State Status. BNA-SUTN AL 2.1.1.

==================================================

Topic: Interstate Agreements Streamlined Sales Tax Conformity to Agreement

Nonmember To date, Alabama has not passed conforming legislation and therefore, cannot seek full membership. 2002 Ala. S.B. 185, effective April 17, 2002. However, as an advisory member, it can participate in work groups and vote on proposals before the State and Local Advisory Council (SLAC). 2002 Ala. S.B. 185, effective April 17, 2002; Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board, Streamlined State Status. BNA-SUTN AL 2.1.2.

==================================================

Topic: Interstate Agreements Multistate Tax Commission

Compact Member Alabama is a compact member of the Multistate Tax Commission. Ala. Code §40-27-1;Multistate Tax Commission, Member States. The purposes of the compact include:• facilitating proper determination of state and local tax liability; • equitably apportioning tax bases and settling disputes; • promoting uniformity; • facilitating taxpayer convenience and compliance in the filing of tax returns and in other phases of tax administration; and • avoiding duplicative taxation. Ala. Code §40-27-1. In conformity with the Multistate Tax Compact, purchasers liable for Alabama use tax on tangible personal property are entitled to full credit for the amount of sales or use taxes paid by the purchaser with respect to the same property to another state and any of its subdivisions. Ala. Code §40-27-1. BNA-SUTN AL 2.2.

==================================================

Topic: Interstate Agreements Other Interstate Agreements

None Alabama is not a member of any other interstate agreements pertaining to sales and use tax. Ala. Code §40-27-1. BNA-SUTN AL 2.3.
