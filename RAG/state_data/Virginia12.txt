State: Virginia



Topic: Interstate Agreements Streamlined Sales Tax Membership Status

Nonmember Virginia is not a Streamlined Sales Tax Agreement member state. Streamlined Sales Tax: State Info. Virginia is therefore an Advisory State to the Governing Board and can speak regarding any matter presented to the Board. Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board, State Status Map. BNA-SUTN VA 2.1.1.

==================================================

Topic: Interstate Agreements Streamlined Sales Tax Conformity to Agreement

Nonmember Virginia has enacted legislation that authorizes it to enter into the Streamlined Sales Tax Agreement, but has not yet adopted the changes to its laws necessary to comply with the Agreement's requirements. 2002 Va. S.B 688. Virginia is therefore an Advisory State to the Governing Board and can speak regarding any matter presented to the Board. Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board, State Status Map. BNA-SUTN VA 2.1.2.

==================================================

Topic: Interstate Agreements Multistate Tax Commission

Nonmember Virginia is not a member of the Multistate Tax Commission. Multistate Tax Commission: Member States. However, Virginia has enacted legislation requiring the Tax Commissioner of the Department of Taxation to take steps necessary for Virginia to become an associate member of the Multistate Tax Commission. 2018 Va. H.B. 373, effective July 1, 2018. BNA-SUTN VA 2.2.

Topic: Interstate Agreements Other Interstate Agreements

Other Agreements Southeastern States Exchange Agreement Virginia is a member of the Southeastern States Exchange Agreement (SSEA). Southeastern Association of Tax Administrators. The Southeastern States Exchange Agreement seeks to enforce sales and use tax compliance from businesses that operate across state lines. Further, the Southeastern Association of Tax Administrators Information Exchange Agreement is a professional organization of tax administrators that promotes fair, equitable, and efficient tax administration. Southeastern Association of Tax Administrators. Washington Metrorail Safety Commission Interstate Compact Effective March 24, 2017, Virginia enacted and entered into the Washington Metrorail Safety Commission Interstate Compact (Compact), an interstate agreement between the District of Columbia, the State of Maryland, and the Commonwealth of Virginia. The Compact was established to comply with the federal requirement that the three jurisdictions create a legal and independent state authority to provide safety oversight of all fixed rail transit facilities in the region. The District of Columbia, Maryland, and Virginia comprise the membership of the Washington Metropolitan Area Transit Authority(WMATA). Va. Code Ann. §33.2-3101, as enacted by 2017 Va. H.B. 2136, effective March 24, 2017; see 49 U.S.C. §5329. Under the Compact, the Metrorail Safety Commission(MSC) is exempt from paying any taxes or assessments on any property or services, acquired or used by the MSC pursuant to the Compact. The Compact provides that the MSC is “at all times” free from taxation in the District of Columbia, Maryland, and Virginia. Va. Code Ann. §33.2-3101, as enacted by 2017 Va. H.B. 2136, Art. V, §E, effective March 24, 2017. The MSC has no authority to levy taxes. Va. Code Ann. §33.2-3101, as enacted by 2017 Va. H.B. 2136, Art. V., §D effective March 24, 2017. BNA-SUTN VA 2.3.
