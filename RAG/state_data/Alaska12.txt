Topic: Interstate Agreements Multistate Tax Commission

Compact Member Alaska is a compact member of the Multistate Tax Commission. However, Alaska does not impose a state sales and use tax. Multistate Tax Commission, Member States. Compact members are states(represented by the heads of the tax agencies administering corporate income and sales and use taxes) that have enacted the Multistate Tax Compact into their state law. These states govern the Commission and participate in a wide range of projects and programs. Multistate Tax Commission, Member States. BNA-SUTN AK 2.2.
