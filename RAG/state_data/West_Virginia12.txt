State: West Virginia



Topic: Interstate Agreements Streamlined Sales Tax Membership Status

Full Member West Virginia is a full member of the Streamlined Sales and Use Tax Agreement(SSUTA). Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board, West Virginia. West Virginia votes on Streamlined Sales and Use Tax amendments and interpretations, and any sellers registering with the Streamlined Sales and Use Tax system are required to collect and remit tax on sales into West Virginia when appropriate. The Tax Commissioner is authorized to enter into agreements that permit federal and state tax collection officials to inspect reports, declarations, or returns filed with the Tax Commissioner in West Virginia. Other jurisdictions involved in these agreements must grant similar privileges to the West Virginia Tax Commissioner or the Attorney General of West Virginia. W. Va. Code ยง11-10-5d(i). West Virginia will relieve sellers and certified service providers from tax liability to West Virginia and its local jurisdictions for having charged and collected the incorrect amount of sales and use tax resulting from the seller's or certified service provider's relying on erroneous data in the tax administration practices section of the Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board Taxability Matrix for West Virginia. W. Va. Code ยง11-15B-36;Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board, Certificate of Compliance, West Virginia(version 2018.0). The purpose of the Agreement is to simplify and modernize sales and use tax administration to substantially reduce the burden of tax compliance through improving state level administration of sales and use tax collections, having uniformity in the state and local tax bases, having uniformity of major tax base definitions, having a central electronic registration system for all member states, simplifying state and local tax rates, having uniform sourcing rules for all taxable transactions, simplifying administration of exemptions, having simplified tax returns, simplifying tax remittances, and protecting consumer privacy. See Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board, About Us; Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board, State Guide to the Streamlined Sales Tax Project (March 1, 2019). BNA-SUTN WV 2.1.1.

Topic: Interstate Agreements Streamlined Sales Tax Conformity to Agreement

In Compliance As a full member of the Streamlined Sales and Use Tax Agreement for which the Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board has not issued a notice of noncompliance, West Virginia is considered to be in full conformity with the Agreement. Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board, Certificate of Compliance, West Virginia (version 2018.0). On Oct. 25, 2017, the Compliance Review and Interpretations Committee issued a report finding that West Virginia is not out-of-compliance with the Agreement. Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board, 2017 Compliance Review Report (Oct. 25, 2017); see also Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board, 2016 Compliance Review Report (Nov. 22, 2016). West Virginia attained compliance and full membership in the Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board on October 1, 2005. 2003 W. Va. H.B. 3014, effective July 1, 2004 (see the Tax Department's Executive Summary of the state's Main Street Fairness Act of 2013); 2004 W. Va. H.B. 4349, effective Jan. 1, 2004;Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board, West Virginia. BNA-SUTN WV 2.1.2.

Topic: Interstate Agreements Other Interstate Agreements

Uniform Exchange of Information Agreement West Virginia has entered into various interstate agreements. See e.g., Federation of Tax Administrators, Uniform Exchange of Information Agreement. Uniform Exchange of Information Agreement The Federation of Tax Administrators developed the Uniform Exchange of Information Agreement (UEIA), which West Virginia has signed, in an effort to facilitate tax administration and improve tax compliance by encouraging the exchange of taxpayer information among states. Federation of Tax Administrators. Southeastern Association of Tax Administrators West Virginia is a member of the Southeastern Association of Tax Administrators (SEATA). Southeastern Association of Tax Administrators is an organization of professional tax administrators that promotes fair, equitable, and efficient tax administration. Southeastern Association of Tax Administrators, SEATA Brochure (Oct. 2007). Reciprocity Agreements Related to Small Businesses The West Virginia Legislature has authorized the governor to enter into new and renew any existing reciprocity agreements related to the regulation and taxation of small businesses that are headquartered in West Virginia and that also conduct business in states sharing contiguous borders with the state or that also conduct business in the District of Columbia. However, any reciprocity agreement affecting taxation may not be entered into unless the agreement is first approved by an act of the West Virginia Legislature. W. Va.
