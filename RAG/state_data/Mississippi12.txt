State: Mississippi



Topic: Interstate Agreements Streamlined Sales Tax Membership Status

Nonmember Although Mississippi is an advisory state of the Streamlined Sales Tax Project, it has not yet enacted legislation to comply with the Streamlined Sales and Use Tax Agreement. Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board, State Info. However, the Mississippi Legislature has enacted legislation permitting the state to enter into an agreement, such as the Streamlined Sales and Use Tax Agreement, with one or more states to substantially reduce the burden of tax compliance for all sellers and for all types of commerce and enact legislation for Mississippi to comply with such an agreement. Topic: Interstate Agreements Streamlined Sales Tax Conformity to Agreement

Nonmember Mississippi is neither a full member nor an associate member of the Streamlined Sales and Use Tax Agreement. Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board, State Info. BNA-SUTN MS 2.1.2.

Topic: Interstate Agreements Multistate Tax Commission

Associate/Project Member Mississippi is an associate and project member of the Multistate Tax Commission. Multistate Tax Commission, Member States. The Multistate Tax Commission is an intergovernmental state tax agency that works on behalf of states and taxpayers to administer equitably and efficiently tax laws that apply to multistate and multinational enterprises. Multistate Tax Commission, About the Multistate Tax Commission. Associate members are states that participate in commission meetings and otherwise consult and cooperate with the commission and its other member states, or participate in commission programs or projects as project members. Multistate Tax Commission, Definition of Member States. BNA-SUTN MS 2.2.

Topic: Interstate Agreements Other Interstate Agreements

None Mississippi is not a party to any other sales and use tax-related interstate agreements. BNA-SUTN MS 2.3.
