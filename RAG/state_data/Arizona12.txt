Topic: Interstate Agreements Multistate Tax Commission

Associate/Project Member Arizona is an associate member of the Multistate Tax Commission. Multistate Tax Commission, Member States. The Arizona Department of Revenue allows a vendor to accept the Multistate Tax Commission's Uniform Sales and Use Tax Certificate for resale purposes. The certificate is not acceptable for purposes of establishing the rights to any other statutory exemption or deduction. Arizona Transaction Privilege Tax Procedure 17-1 (April 1, 2017). For more information about exemption certificates, see the Sales and Use Tax Navigator, at Arizona 31.8. BNA-SUTN AZ 2.2.
