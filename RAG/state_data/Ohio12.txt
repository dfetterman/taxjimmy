State: Ohio



Topic: Interstate Agreements Streamlined Sales Tax Membership Status

Full Member On March 24, 2003, Ohio enacted H.B. 95 to bring the state into compliance with the Streamlined Sales and Use Tax Agreement. 2003 Ohio H.B. 95, effective July 1, 2003; see also Ohio Rev. Code Ann. §5740.04. The majority of the streamlined provisions took effect July 1, 2003. 2003 Ohio H.B. 95, effective July 1, 2003; see also Ohio Rev. Code Ann. §5740.04. On April 27, 2005, Ohio filed with the Streamlined Sales Tax System its Petition for Membership and a Certificate of Compliance. It filed a revised Certificate of Compliance on June 8, 2005. Ohio gained full membership in the Governing Board on Oct. 29, 2013, after unanimous approval by the Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board. Ohio's full membership became effective Jan. 1, 2014. Ohio was an associate member of the Governing Board from July 1, 2005 to Dec. 31, 2013. Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board, Press Release: Ohio is Now a Full Member State Effective January 1, 2014 (Nov. 5, 2013). A state that is a full member is in compliance with the Streamlined Sales and Use Tax Agreement through its laws, rules, regulations, and policies. A state that is an associate member of the Streamlined Sales Tax System is in compliance with the Streamlined Sales and Use Tax Agreement except that the laws, rules, regulations, and policies to bring the state into compliance are not yet in effect but are scheduled to take effect no later than 12 months after becoming an associate member. Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board, Certificate of Compliance, Ohio (version 2018.0); Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board, Taxability Matrix, Ohio (version 2018.0); Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board, State Guide to the Streamlined Sales Tax Project (March 1, 2019). For information about which Ohio laws are effective and in compliance with the Streamlined Sales and Use Tax Agreement, see Ohio's Certificate of Compliance and Taxability Matrix. Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board, Certificate of Compliance, Ohio (version 2018.0); Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board, Taxability Matrix, Ohio (version 2018.0). BNA-SUTN OH 2.1.1.

Topic: Interstate Agreements Streamlined Sales Tax Conformity to Agreement

In Compliance As a full member of the Streamlined Sales and Use Tax Agreement for which the Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board has not issued a notice of noncompliance, Ohio is considered to be in full conformity with the Agreement. Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board, Certificate of Compliance, Ohio (version 2018.0). On Oct. 25, 2017, the Compliance Review and Interpretations Committee issued a report finding that Ohio was not out-of-compliance with the Agreement. Though the committee noted that Ohio did not provide sufficient notification of a sales tax holiday as required in Section 322 of the Agreement, it ultimately voted not to find the state out-of-compliance. Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board, 2017 Compliance Review Report (Oct. 25, 2017); see also Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board, 2016 Compliance Review Report (Nov. 22, 2016). As a member state, Ohio has changed its sales tax administration laws so they fully comply with the provisions of the Streamlined Sales Tax Agreement. Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board, Certificate of Compliance, Ohio (version 2018.0); Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board, Taxability Matrix, Ohio (version 2018.0); Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board, Frequently Asked Questions. BNA-SUTN OH 2.1.2.

Topic: Interstate Agreements Multistate Tax Commission

Associate/Project Member Ohio is an associate and project member of the Multistate Tax Commission. Multistate Tax Commission, Member States. States such as Ohio that are associate members participate in meetings and otherwise consult and cooperate with the commission and its other member states or participate in commission programs or projects as members. Multistate Tax Commission, Member States. BNA-SUTN OH 2.2.

No Ohio is not a member of other interstate agreements pertaining to sales and use tax. Ohio Rev. Code Ann. §5739.02.
