State: Illinois



Topic: Interstate Agreements Streamlined Sales Tax Membership Status

Nonmember Although Illinois legislation authorizes it to enter into the Streamlined Sales and Use Tax Agreement, it has not yet adopted the legislative changes necessary to comply with the Streamlined Sales and Use Tax Agreement's requirements. 35 ILCS 171/5. Therefore, Illinois is merely an Advisor State to the Governing Board. Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board, State Status Map. BNA-SUTN IL 2.1.1.

==================================================

Topic: Interstate Agreements Streamlined Sales Tax Conformity to Agreement

Nonmember Illinois has not yet enacted legislation necessary to comply with the requirements of the Streamlined Sales and Use Tax Agreement. 35 ILCS 171/5. BNA-SUTN IL 2.1.2.

==================================================

Topic: Interstate Agreements Multistate Tax Commission

Associate/Project Member Illinois is an associate and project member of the Multistate Tax Commission. Multistate Tax Commission, Member States. BNA-SUTN IL 2.2.

Topic: Interstate Agreements Other Interstate Agreements

Yes The Illinois Department of Revenue has the authority to exchange information that is necessary for tax administration with any state, local subdivision of any state, and the federal government. 20 ILCS 2505/2505-65. BNA-SUTN IL 2.3.
