Topic: Interstate Agreements Multistate Tax Commission

Compact Member The State of Idaho is a compact member of the Multistate Tax Commission. Idaho Code ยง63-3701;Multistate Tax Commission, Member States. The Multistate Tax Commission, which was created by the Multistate Tax Compact, is an intergovernmental state tax agency that works on behalf of states and taxpayers to equitably and efficiently administer tax laws that apply to multistate and multinational enterprises. Multistate Tax Commission, About the Multistate Tax Commission. Compact member states that are represented by the heads of the tax agencies that administer corporate income and sales and use taxes are states that have enacted the Multistate Tax Compact into their state law. Compact member states govern the Commission and participate in a wide range of projects and programs. Multistate Tax Commission, Definition of Member States. The Multistate Tax Commission is charged with facilitating the proper determination of state and local tax liability of multistate taxpayers, including the equitable apportionment of tax bases and settlement of apportionment disputes. Additionally, the Commission promotes uniformity or compatibility in significant components of tax systems and facilitates taxpayer convenience and compliance in the filing of tax returns and in other phases of tax administration. Multistate Tax Commission, About the Multistate Tax Commission. BNA-SUTN ID 2.2.
