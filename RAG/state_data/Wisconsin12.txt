The purpose of the Agreement is to simplify and modernize sales and use tax administration to substantially reduce the burden of tax compliance through improving state level administration of sales and use tax collections, having uniformity in the state and local tax bases, having uniformity of major tax base definitions, having a central electronic registration system for all member states, simplifying state and local tax rates, having uniform sourcing rules for all taxable transactions, simplifying administration of exemptions, having simplified tax returns, simplifying tax remittances, and protecting consumer privacy. Topic: Interstate Agreements Streamlined Sales Tax Conformity to Agreement

In Compliance As a full member of the Streamlined Sales and Use Tax Agreement for which the Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board has not issued a notice of noncompliance, Wisconsin is considered to be in full conformity with the Agreement. Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board, Certificate of Compliance, Wisconsin (version 2018.0). On Oct. 25, 2017, the Compliance Review and Interpretations Committee issued a report finding that Wisconsin is not out-of-compliance with the Agreement. Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board, 2017 Compliance Review Report (Oct. 25, 2017); see also Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board, 2016 Compliance Review Report (Nov. 22, 2016). BNA-SUTN WI 2.1.2.

Topic: Interstate Agreements Multistate Tax Commission

Associate Member Wisconsin is an Associate Member of the Multistate Tax Commission. As such it is entitled to participate in meetings, programs and projects of the commission and may consult with the commission and other member states. Multistate Tax Commission, Member States. BNA-SUTN WI 2.2.
