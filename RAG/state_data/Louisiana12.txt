State: Louisiana



Topic: Interstate Agreements Streamlined Sales Tax Membership Status

Nonmember Louisiana has not entered into the Streamlined Sales and Use Tax Agreement. See La. Rev. Stat. Ann. §47:335.4. However, it has passed legislation authorizing the Department of Revenue to enter into the Agreement, under certain conditions. La. Rev. Stat. Ann. §47:335.4; La. Rev. Stat. Ann. §47:335.6. Sales Tax Streamlining and Modernization Commission In 2015, Louisiana established the Sales Tax Streamlining and Modernization Commission to comprehensively study Louisiana's state and local sales tax systems and to recommend revised practices to modernize the system. La. Rev. Stat. Ann. §47:301.2(B), as enacted by 2015 La. H.B. 471, effective Aug. 1, 2015. The commission's duty is to perform a comprehensive study of four issues relating to Louisiana's state and local sales tax systems and to develop specific recommendations for legislative consideration. La. Rev. Stat. Ann. §47:301.2(E), as enacted by 2015 La. H.B. 471, effective Aug. 1, 2015. Those topics include how sales tax policy affects Louisiana, La. Rev. Stat. Ann. §47:301.2(E)(1), as enacted by 2015 La. H.B. 471, effective Aug. 1, 2015. a comparison of tax bases and rates, La. Rev. Stat. Ann. §47:301.2(E)(2), as enacted by 2015 La. H.B. 471, effective Aug. 1, 2015. a study of all special tax treatment, La. Rev. Stat. Ann. §47:301.2(E)(3), as enacted by 2015 La. H.B. 471, effective Aug. 1, 2015. and a study of sales tax collection and audit procedures. La. Rev. Stat. Ann. §47:301.2(E)(4), as enacted by 2015 La. H.B. 471, effective Aug. 1, 2015. The commission includes a subcommittee to recommend potential policy changes regarding coordinated multi-parish sales and use tax audits. The subcommittee is required to present a report to the commission by Jan. 15, 2017. La. Rev. Stat. Ann. §47:301.2(F), as enacted by 2015 La. H.B. 471, effective Aug. 1, 2015, as amended by 2016 La. H.B. 1060, effective Aug. 1, 2016. After having conducted its comprehensive review, the commission must prepare a recommendation report. A recommendation requires approval of three-quarters of the commission's members. The commission's interim report is to be submitted to the governor, the president of the Senate, and the speaker of the House of Representatives no later than Jan. 15, 2016. The final report is to be prepared as determined by the commission. La. Rev. Stat. Ann. §47:301.2(G), as enacted by 2015 La. H.B. 471, effective Aug. 1, 2015. The commission terminates on June 30, 2017. La. Rev. Stat. Ann. §47:301.2(G), as enacted by 2015 La. H.B. 471, effective Aug. 1, 2015. However, if two-thirds of its members vote in favor of continuing its work prior to June 30, 2017, then the commission may continue through June 30, 2018. La. Rev. Stat. Ann. §47:301.2(G), as enacted by 2015 La. H.B. 471, effective Aug. 1, 2015, as amended by 2016 La. H.B. 1060, effective Aug. 1, 2016. BNA-SUTN LA 2.1.1.

Topic: Interstate Agreements Streamlined Sales Tax Conformity to Agreement

Nonmember Louisiana is not a member of the Streamlined Sales and Use Tax Agreement and has not conformed its tax laws to the Agreement. La. Rev. Stat. Ann. §47:335.6. BNA-SUTN LA 2.1.2.

Topic: Interstate Agreements Multistate Tax Commission

Sovereignty Member Louisiana is a Sovereignty Member of the Multistate Tax Commission. Multistate Tax Commission, Member States. BNA-SUTN LA 2.2.

Topic: Interstate Agreements Other Interstate Agreements

SEATA Louisiana is a member of the Southeastern Association of Tax Administrators(SEATA) and participates in the SEATA Information Exchange Program. Louisiana Tax Guide R-20207BR (December 2011). The purpose of the agreement is to facilitate tax administration and compliance across state boundaries. Louisiana encourages its businesses to voluntarily register to collect sales tax in any other state in which they do business. SEATA members collect and exchange sales tax information on behalf of other states and provide this information with other members. Louisiana Tax Guide R-20207BR (December 2011). BNA-SUTN LA 2.3.
