State: Kentucky



Topic: Interstate Agreements Streamlined Sales Tax Membership Status

Full Member Kentucky is a full member of the Streamlined Sales Tax Agreement. Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board, Important Documents, Kentucky. On March 18, 2003, Kentucky enacted H.B. 293 to bring the state into compliance with the Streamlined Sales and Use Tax Agreement, effective July 1, 2004. Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board, Important Documents, Kentucky; Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board, Taxability Matrix, Kentucky (version 2018.0). Kentucky became a full member of the Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board on October 1, 2005. Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board, Important Documents, Kentucky; Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board, Taxability Matrix, Kentucky (version 2018.0). As no city or municipality in Kentucky imposes a local sales or use tax, legislation dealing with state-level administration and a uniform state and local tax base is not necessary to comply with the Agreement. Ky. Rev. Stat. Ann. ยง92.281. BNA-SUTN KY 2.1.1.

Topic: Interstate Agreements Streamlined Sales Tax Conformity to Agreement

In Compliance As a full member of the Streamlined Sales and Use Tax Agreement for which the Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board has not issued a notice of noncompliance, Kentucky is considered to be in full conformity with the Agreement. Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board, Certificate of Compliance, Kentucky (version 2018.0). On Oct. 25, 2017, the Compliance Review and Interpretations Committee issued a report finding that Kentucky is not out-of-compliance with the Agreement. Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board, 2017 Compliance Review Report (Oct. 25, 2017); see also Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board, 2016 Compliance Review Report (Nov. 22, 2016). Kentucky became a full member of the Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board on Oct. 1, 2005. See Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board, Important Documents, Kentucky. Kentucky relieves sellers and certified service providers from tax liability to Kentucky and its local jurisdictions for having charged and collected the incorrect amount of sales and use tax resulting from the seller or certified service provider relying on the Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board Taxability Matrix for Kentucky. Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board, Certificate of Compliance, Kentucky (version 2018.0). Additionally, when Kentucky changes its library of definitions or tax administration practices section of the Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board Taxability Matrix, Kentucky provides sellers and certified service providers with liability relief for the interest and penalties for having charged and collected the incorrect tax until the first day of the calendar month that is at least 30 days after notice of the change to the Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board Taxability Matrix for Kentucky is submitted to the governing board, provided the seller or certified service provider relied on the prior version of the taxability matrix. Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board, Taxability Matrix, Kentucky (version 2018.0); see also Streamlined Sales Tax Governing Board, State Guide to the Streamlined Sales Tax Project (March 1, 2019). BNA-SUTN KY 2.1.2.
